    Welcome to Voxel Playground

    Voxel Playground is a micro-voxel physics sandbox built for VR, offering an immersive environment where almost everything is destructible and interactive.

    This manual serves as your comprehensive guide to the game's mechanics, and more importantly, its powerful modding capabilities. Whether you want to design new cities, craft unique weapons, or create giant characters, Voxel Playground gives you the tools to bring your voxel dreams to life.
